Logging a Complaint


The Complaint tool enables users to log a complaint about the Rights of Way in Snowdonia and search for previously logged complaints.


The ‘Log Complaint’ tool opens a form in the left panel of the webpage. The form has two parts:


  • Log Complaint
  • Search Complaints

In the ‘Log Complaint’ section your name and address have been automatically been entered.


  1. To log a complaint, first locate the problem on the map
  2. On the form, type in a description of the problem in the ‘Nature of Complaint / Enquiry’ box
  3. Scroll down and type in a description of the location in the ‘Descriptive Location’ box
  4. Click on the ‘Pin’ button and click on the map to indicate the location of the problem. A pin marker will be displayed on the map to mark the location.
  5. Ensure all the details are correct and click on the ‘Submit’ button. A message will be displayed to confirm that the complaint was successfully logged with a reference ID.
  6. If, before submitting the complaint, the details and/ or the location on the map is wrong, click on ‘New’ to reset the form.
  7. To log additional problems / enquiries, click on ‘New’

To search for a existing or previous complaint


  1. Click on ‘Search Complaints’
  2. Enter the reference / complaint ID and click on the ‘Search’ button
  3. The details of the complaint will be displayed with a link to ‘Locate on Map’ to view the complaint location on map.
  4. Click on the ‘Locate on Map’ link to display the location of the complaint / enquiry on the map
